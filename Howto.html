How it Works

Frequently Asked

DO I NEED A BUSINESS LICENSE TO OPEN A SHOP ON BOBLA?

BOBLA doesn’t require sellers to have a business license to sell on our marketplace. However, when selling on BOBLA you're required to follow any laws that apply to you as a small business selling online. If you have any questions regarding which laws apply to you, please contact a qualified professional.

WHAT ARE THE FEES AND TAXES FOR SELLING ON BOBLA?

BOBLA charges a 15 cent listing fee and 7% on the total item price. This includes any shipping and gift wrapping costs that you have charged the customer.

HOW DO I OPEN A BOBLA SHOP?

After filling out a seller application, a BOBLA rep will contact you via email to verify your identify. This is a quality control method that we've implemented to protect the integrity of our brand. If our missions align, our rep will send you a seller guide. You will find everything you need to get started in the seller handbook.

HOW DO I GET PAID ON BOBLA?

BOBLA only accepts PayPal at the moment. BOBLA sellers will get paid via PayPal or bank transfer on the 1st and 15th of each month.

WHAT DO I DO AFTER I SELL AN ITEM?

After you make a sale on BOBLA, you can find all of the order details in your account. BOBLA also sends a notification to the BOBLA Xpress app and an email to the address on file. You may want to add transaction@bobla.io to your contacts, so these emails don't go to your spam folder.

WHAT ABOUT SHOP POLICIES?

BOBLA sellers set their own policies because it's your shop, so your rules. Remember to list out all shop policies such as shipping, returns/exchanges, payments, damaged items, customized items, and any other important guidelines you may think your customers need to know.
